Gabriel Schener’s new book, NHibernate 3 Beginner’s Guide was published today. Go check it out. Gabriel knows NHibernate inside and out – certainly better than me – and worked hard to give the community the best book possible. For technical reviewers, he was backed up by two of the best:Fabio Maulo and Jose Romaniello.

Disclosure: Gabriel was an amazing technical reviewer on my book, NHibernate 3.0 Cookbook, along with Jose, Fabio, and Tuna Toksoz. Packt Publishing published both NHibernate 3 Beginner’s Guide and NHibernate 3.0 Cookbook.
